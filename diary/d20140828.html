自备早餐的第一天2014-08-28

Back

It's the first day that I prepare my lunch myself.

Formerly, it's my GF's Mum who prepare my lunch, and yesterday, she went back home, it means that, in the future days, it should be myself who do the preparing.

Woke up at 7:10 AM, almost the same time with S, she went to wash, and I began to cook. It's the first time cooking in the morning, so, the preparation is not enough, I spent about 10 minutes on searching cookbook, finally decided to cook the <Chili and sour potato>. Then washing and cutting vegetable costs almost 15 minutes. Cooking costs 5 minutes. Mentionable, in the cooking precedure, I put not enough oil, so some potato clinged to the bottom of the pan. It's not good, but fine. And another thing, the rice is not enough, either. I should put more rice the next time.

Finish cooking at about 7:50 ＡＭ, wash face and rinse teeth in a hurry, wear clothes, and leave at about 8:10 AM. Got on the bus at about 8:40 AM(need to wait for the bus). And arrived company at about 9:30 AM.

So, finally, if I prepare my lunch myself, before arriving company, it needs about 2.5 hours in the morning. Not too short, not too long~ I need to get used to this life rhythm. And I believe it will be improved in the future.
